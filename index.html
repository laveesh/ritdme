About the Conference

RITDME- 2018 is being organized to bring researchers and experts from academia on a common platform to address the opportunities and challenges in the field of Science & Technology. The conference will provide an opportunity for presentation advances in experimental and analytical research in the field of Mechanical Engineering.
The conference are being structured to cover Energy, Mechatronics, Automation, CAD/CAM, Advance Materials, Green Manufacturing, Nanotechnology to explore the areas in the field of Mechanical Engineering.

Who should attend?
Researchers, academicians, scientists, practicing engineers and industry professionals to showcase and deliberate about their research work, studies, contributions, developments and new concepts related to the field of Mechanical Engineering.

Why Attend?

  • Network with hundreds of other students, academics, researchers and professionals involved in mechanical engineering.
  • To know recent updates in the relevant fields from the expert practitioners.
  • Enhance your expertise set with the conference and see the work.
  • Explore and debate topics, discuss in formal and informal settings.
  • Present your work and get feedback
  • Promote your work.
